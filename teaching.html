Teaching & Students

I always look for working with and financially supporting highly motivated, hard-working, and well-qualified students who have a genuine interest in Trustworthy AI research and applications.

Interested candidates are encouraged to contact me by email with a resume and transcripts. Strong background in mathematics and programming is desirable. I financially support qualifying PhD students with research assistantships.
